audio

headphones

  • over-ear: Sony XM5
    • I love these headphones! They are very comfortable, have amazing sound quality, and excellent noise cancelling. These were my replacement for the Sony 910N. My only gripe is that they do not fold, so it takes up a lot of space in a backpack or bag.
  • earbuds: Apple AirPods 3
    • The best earbuds I have owned so far. I still use my old Apple Airpods Pro (original) for the gym, as the silicone ear tips stay in my ear better.
