why this site exists

For years I have been scribbling thoughts in paper journals or sticky notes that somehow always disappear the exact moment I need them.

This site is my notebook that will never be left on a train.

I have always been somewhat of an analog person, not really accompanying trends or being big on social media.

Then I found out about digital gardens, which are personal sites that are not built to go viral, but to slowly grow ideas in public, mostly for yourself.

That idea felt like coming home. So here we are (although my website isn’t purely a digital garden. read: what is this website?).

This site was founded on 00V13.
